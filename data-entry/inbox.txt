Mythical Monsters
Mythical Monsters are a special class of Monsters that appear across multiple Islands. There are seven Monsters that manifest the Mythical (Mythical Element) Element, which are found on the five Natural Islands and both Fire Islands.
All Mythicals from the Natural and Fire Islands can be teleported to Mythical Island once they reach level 15, a special Island featuring every Mythical, including new Mythicals exclusive to the Island. Cataliszt, the Dream (Dream Element) Single-Elemental, can breed with the Mythicals found on the Natural Islands to create brand-new Dreamythicals, hybrids of the Dream (Dream Element) and Mythical (Mythical Element) Elements.

History
The Mythical class was originally exclusive to the PlayStation®Vita version of My Singing Monsters. At the time, the class was known as "PlayStation Vita Exclusive" and its only members were G'joob and Yawstrich. The content exclusive to this version of the game remained so due to an exclusivity contract Big Blue Bubble Inc. had with Sony Interactive Entertainment.

After support for the PlayStation®Vita ended in March 2019, the exclusivity deal expired, and the PlayStation®Vita Exclusive Monsters and Decorations became available in the main version of the game on September 5th, 2019.

Throughout June 2022, an event called Month of the Mythical took place. In this event, the community could vote on various polls to help the Monster-Handlers develop the Cold Island Mythical. The result was Strombonin, which was revealed and released in Anniversary Month 2022.

People who pledged for the Ultimate Creator Experience on the My Singing Monsters: The Board Game Kickstarter had an all-expenses-paid trip to Big Blue Bubble headquarters to design their very own Mythical Monster. These Mythicals, in order of release, are as follows:

On November 23rd, 2022, Cherubble was released on Fire Oasis and Mythical Island. Its concept was created by EriktheCleric.

On February 15th, 2023, Hyehehe was released on Earth Island and Mythical Island. Its concept was created by QuinnQwerty.

On April 5th, 2023, Anglow was released on Water Island and Mythical Island. Its concept was created by Chris Wimberly (ChrisShark).

The Monster-Handlers have stated that Anglow was the last of the Ultimate Creator Experience Mythicals, and the last Mythical on Fire Haven would not be designed by an Ultimate Creator (possibly due to the person who pledged the creation of the Fire Haven Mythical opting out). The Fire Haven Mythical, known as Buzzinga, was instead designed with the community by voting on various polls to determine its characteristics during the Month of the Mythical in June 2023, just like Strombonin. This time around, though, the community was not able to vote on the type of musical sound it plays, having already been chosen beforehand by the Monster-Handlers.

On September 6th, 2023, Buzzinga was released on Fire Haven and Mythical Island. It was created by the community through a series of polls held by the Monster-Handlers on their social media pages.

List of Mythical Monsters
Mythical Singles can be split into two groups: Core Mythicals, which all have the Mythical Element, and Cataliszt, which has the Dream Element. Each Core Mythical can be obtained by breeding two specific Monsters together at any time of the year. Throughout the year, there can be Mythical Breeding Bonanzas that increase the odds of successfully breeding a Core Mythical Monster.

About
Mythical Island Mythical Island Icon is a teleporting Island unlocked after purchasing Fire Oasis.

It hosts all Mythical Monsters together in a single Island. When a Mythical Monster reaches Level 15, it can be teleported to this Island. They will reset their Level, and get sent to the Nursery, and will also stay there permanently, unless sold.

On this Island, Core Mythicals found on the Natural and Fire Islands can breed with the exclusive Dream Single-Elemental Cataliszt to create brand new 'Dreamythicals' exclusive to Mythical Island. Each Mythical has its own Dreamythical counterpart.

Mythical Island was released on September 28th, 2022 in Version 3.7.1.

Song
Official_Mythical_Island_Full_Song
Official Mythical Island Full Song

Composer(s): Tom Meikle

Mythical Island’s song alternates between two main speeds: The first and fourth major portions of the song are in 165 beats per minute in 3/4 time, and the second portion and the transitional parts are in 110 beats per minute in 4/4 time. The third major portion has Monsters playing in both tempos, creating a polyrhythm. The song is in D Major, D Mixolydian, D Harmonic Minor and D Lydian.

Mythical Island has an ambience that consists of wind and dream-like sparkles.

Breeding
On Mythical Island, the main difference in breeding mechanics between this Island and the Natural and Fire Islands is that Mythical Monsters can now breed, including their Rare variants.

Single-Element Mythical Monsters can only be bred with Cataliszt to make Dreamythical Monsters, but breeding Monsters on Mythical Island can potentially gain breeding failures. Unlike Single-Element Mythicals, Dreamythicals are incapable of breeding with other Monsters.

Single Element Monsters cannot be bred and must either be bought from the Market or teleported from their Natural/Fire Island. Dreamythicals are bred by breeding Cataliszt and their corresponding Mythical Monster. Breeding Dreamythicals is not a guaranteed success.

Teleport
Teleport Icon
Some Monsters can be teleported to another island when they are fed to a certain level. For most islands, the minimum level required for teleporting a Monster is level 15, with the exceptions of The Colossingum, which only requires level 5, and the Ethereal Islets, which only require level 14. When a Monster is teleported, it permanently leaves the island it was born on and is moved to the new island, turning back into an egg and going into the empty Nursery or Egg Cup on the destination island. This also resets the Monster's level down to 1 and removes the Monster from Gold Island, if it is placed there.

At present, this process cannot be reversed. That is, after a Monster is teleported to another island, it can't be returned to its original home.

Teleporting may also change a Monster's breeding and incubation times, as the destination islands have different features than the ones of the home islands, and may enable other characteristics not available on the monster's birthplace. However, a monster's nickname and costumes will remain unchanged on the new island.

Teleporting eligible Monsters is also feasible on the Mirror Islands, which follow the same teleporting rules of the islands that they mirror. This makes Mirror Island breeding a great way to obtain Monsters to teleport, if one is preoccupied with breeding on the Natural or Magical Islands.

The Colossingum Monsters
As of Version 4.1.0, all 30 common Natural Monsters, the Single and Double Element Ethereals, and the 5 Core Seasonals can be teleported to The Colossingum upon reaching Level 5. These monsters can be teleported from any of the islands they are available on, which includes the Natural and Mirror Islands, the Fire Islands, the Magical Islands, Shugabush Island, Seasonal Shanty, Ethereal Island, and the Ethereal Islets. Monsters cannot be teleported anywhere after they are placed on The Colossingum.

Magicals
Transposing is a mechanic in My Singing Monsters, introduced in Version 4.2.0, used to obtain Monsters on Magical Nexus and single element Paironormals on Paironormal Carnival. Transposing is similar to Teleporting. In order to be Transposed, a Monster on must be fed to at least Level 18, and Currency must be also spent. When a Monster is Transposed, it will be sent to the Stair Shaper on Magical Nexus / "Nursery" of the Beyond on the Paironormal Carnival depending on what you're transposing.
With Paironormals, you can transpose both forms, however you can only hatch them in the same island mode the monster resonates with (Ex. transposing a Minor Owlesque will only be able to be placed on the Minor Mode of Paironormal Carnival). Looking at the egg from the opposite mode shows a yellow (Major Mode) / blue (Minor Mode) egg that looks similar to the Paironormal Projections and can't be hatched on that side.

Alterations in Breeding Mechanics
As the monsters' breeding capabilities may be altered after Teleportation, you may not be able to use the same breeding combinations available on the Natural Islands to obtain a Rare Monster. For example, on Cold Island, you can breed a Furcorn and a Quibble to get a Deedge. However, after the Furcorn and Quibble are teleported to Shugabush Island, they cannot be bred together; all breeding combinations on Shugabush Island must include Shugabush itself. A Deedge must either be fed to Level 15 on Cold or Mirror Cold Island and teleported or bought from the Market.

The same change does not occur on Ethereal Island, Mythical Island, or Seasonal Shanty, as Ethereals, Mythicals, and Seasonals can't be used for breeding on their original islands in the first place.
