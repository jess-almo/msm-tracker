About
Light Island Light Island Icon is available to purchase for 1,000,000 Coins Coins after unlocking Fire Haven. It is a Magical Island, featuring Magical Monsters and manifesting the Light Element Light Element. It was added to the game on September 30th, 2020 during Version 3.0.1. It is home to 59 Monsters and 97 Costumes.

Song
Composer(s): Dave Kerr, Christopher Pollard, Sam Clark (Light Element Monsters, Dipster Portrait), Tom Meikle (Whiz-bang Portrait, Yelmut Portrait, Tiawa Portrait, Drummidary Portrait), Dylan Cole (Yelmut Portrait, Tiawa Portrait, Drummidary Portrait)

Light Island’s song plays at a speed of 80 beats per minute and is in the key of B♭ major.

Light Island's core chord progression is based on three types of 4-bar cadential blocks interspersed throughout the song. Block A (i.e., Mammott's, Potbelly's, and Kayna's first track) consists of a prolonged B♭ chord. Block B (i.e., track 2) consists of a IV-I cadence (two bars of E♭ followed by two bars of B♭). Block C (i.e., track 3) consists of a V-I cadence (two bars of F followed by two bars of B♭). Altogether, they make up a variant of what is commonly known as the “12-Bar Blues”.

Non-chord tones are explored in a variety of contexts, and are prevalent in the majority of Light Island monsters, including ones expected to be responsible for chord expression.


The song is also available to download via the My Singing Monsters Soundtrack volume 2.

Pattern 2: Eb, Eb, Bb, Bb

Indigenous Monsters
The Monsters of Light Island are of the Plant (Plant Element), Cold (Cold Element), Fire (Fire Element), Light (Light Element), Depths (Depths Element), SkyPainting (SkyPainting Element), Dipster (Dipster Element), and Titansoul (Titansoul Element) elements. Since Light Island lacks the Air (Air Element), Water (Water Element), and Earth (Earth Element) elements, no Monster with those elements can exist on it (except for Yelmut, Tiawa, and Drummidary, who are not obtained through breeding). Just as with Fire Haven and Fire Oasis, there is also a difference with Monsters that have the Fire Element, as they take longer to breed and are more difficult to obtain because of their long absence from the Monster World. This also applies to the Magical Monsters.

Breeding
Breeding on Light Island is relatively simple, requiring two Monsters with the target Monsters elements. Multiple combinations can obtain the same Monster, so for simplicity, only one combination will be listed per Monster. Other combinations can be found on that Monster's page.

Common
Single Element Monsters cannot be bred and must be bought from the Market. Doubles are bred with two Singles, Triples are bred with a Single and a Double, and Quads can be bred with two Doubles or a Triple and a Single, with the latter being more effective.

About
Light Island Light Island Icon is available to purchase for 1,000,000 Coins Coins after unlocking Fire Haven. It is a Magical Island, featuring Magical Monsters and manifesting the Light Element Light Element. It was added to the game on September 30th, 2020 during Version 3.0.1. It is home to 59 Monsters and 97 Costumes.

Song
Composer(s): Dave Kerr, Christopher Pollard, Sam Clark (Light Element Monsters, Dipster Portrait), Tom Meikle (Whiz-bang Portrait, Yelmut Portrait, Tiawa Portrait, Drummidary Portrait), Dylan Cole (Yelmut Portrait, Tiawa Portrait, Drummidary Portrait)

Light Island’s song plays at a speed of 80 beats per minute and is in the key of B♭ major.

Light Island's core chord progression is based on three types of 4-bar cadential blocks interspersed throughout the song. Block A (i.e., Mammott's, Potbelly's, and Kayna's first track) consists of a prolonged B♭ chord. Block B (i.e., track 2) consists of a IV-I cadence (two bars of E♭ followed by two bars of B♭). Block C (i.e., track 3) consists of a V-I cadence (two bars of F followed by two bars of B♭). Altogether, they make up a variant of what is commonly known as the “12-Bar Blues”.

Non-chord tones are explored in a variety of contexts, and are prevalent in the majority of Light Island monsters, including ones expected to be responsible for chord expression.


The song is also available to download via the My Singing Monsters Soundtrack volume 2.

Indigenous Monsters
The Monsters of Light Island are of the Plant (Plant Element), Cold (Cold Element), Fire (Fire Element), Light (Light Element), Depths (Depths Element), SkyPainting (SkyPainting Element), Dipster (Dipster Element), and Titansoul (Titansoul Element) elements. Since Light Island lacks the Air (Air Element), Water (Water Element), and Earth (Earth Element) elements, no Monster with those elements can exist on it (except for Yelmut, Tiawa, and Drummidary, who are not obtained through breeding). Just as with Fire Haven and Fire Oasis, there is also a difference with Monsters that have the Fire Element, as they take longer to breed and are more difficult to obtain because of their long absence from the Monster World. This also applies to the Magical Monsters.

Breeding
Breeding on Light Island is relatively simple, requiring two Monsters with the target Monsters elements. Multiple combinations can obtain the same Monster, so for simplicity, only one combination will be listed per Monster. Other combinations can be found on that Monster's page.

Common
Single Element Monsters cannot be bred and must be bought from the Market. Doubles are bred with two Singles, Triples are bred with a Single and a Double, and Quads can be bred with two Doubles or a Triple and a Single, with the latter being more effective.

Description
Yelmut is a Quad-Element Fire Monster that is found on Light Island and Amber Island. It was added alongside Epic Boskus on February 9th, 2022, during version 3.4.0. As a Quad-Element Fire Monster on a Magical Island, Yelmut is only available at select times. When available, it is obtained by purchasing in the Market for 300 Relics (Relics). By default, its incubation time is 1 day and 16 hours long. Yelmut has a high Coin production on Light Island, comparable to Blow't and Tiawa. The Goal for placing your first Yelmut rewards the player with 100,000 Coins (Coins) and 4 Relics.

On Amber Island, Yelmut is only available at select times. When available, it is obtained by purchasing in the Market for 30 Relics as a Vessel, which then can be revitalized by zapping eggs into it. As a Fire Quad-Elemental, Yelmut has an excellent Relic production.

Yelmut's song is a powerful vocal ensemble consisting of prolonged notes in a "loose" harmony.

On Amber Island, Yelmut's sound is identical to its sound on the Continent. However, it has been pitch shifted up to a B natural, unlike the B flat in its original sound. It sings alongside Floogull, Glowl, Stogg, and others in the sections before PongPing's parts.

On Light Island, Yelmut sings alongside the rest of the Monsters (except Furcorn, Fluoress, and the Dipsters) near the end of the song.

On Mirror Light Island, Yelmut plays at the beginning of the song with Pluckbill, Flowah, Drummidary, and Sooza.

Breeding
Unlike in Dawn of Fire, Yelmut cannot be bred. Instead, it must be purchased from the Market. On Amber Island, it must be purchased as an inactive Vessel and revitalized by zapping eggs.

Description
Tiawa is a Quad-Element Fire Monster that is found on Light Island and Amber Island. It was added alongside Candelavra on August 3rd, 2022, during version 3.6.0. As a Quad-Element Fire Monster on a Magical Island, Tiawa is only available at select times. When available, it is obtained by purchasing in the Market for 300 Relics. By default, its incubation time is 1 day and 16 hours long. Tiawa has a high Coin production on Light Island, comparable to Yelmut and Blow't. The Goal for placing your first Tiawa rewards the player with 100,000 Coins (Coins) and 4 Relics (Relics).

On Amber Island, Tiawa is only available at select times. When available, it is obtained by purchasing in the Market for 30 Relics as a Vessel, which then can be revitalized by zapping eggs into it. As a Fire Quad-Elemental, Tiawa has an excellent Relic production.

Song
Audio Sample: 

Instrument: Marimba

Like its Dawn of Fire counterpart, Tiawa plays a marimba with an occasional trill. On Amber Island, it plays alongside Woolabee and PongPing, in both the first verse with Wynq and the second verse with Flowah.

On Light Island, it plays alongside Fluoress, Furcorn, and the Dipsters, as well as near the end of the song with Yelmut, Drummidary, and Pluckbill.

On Mirror Light Island, it plays in verses with TooToo, Boskus, and Potbelly, and cuts out right before the very end.

Description
Drummidary is a Quint-Element Fire Monster that is found on Light Island and Amber Island. It was added alongside Rare Krillby on February 8th, 2023, during version 3.8.2. As a Quint-Element Fire Monster on a Magical Island, Drummidary is only available at select times. When available, it is obtained by purchasing in the Market for 500 Relics (Relics). By default, its incubation time is 3 days and 8 hours long. As a Quint-Elemental, Drummidary has excellent Coin production on Light Island, , although Quads and their variants are more efficient in maximum income per bed. The Goal for placing your first Drummidary rewards the player with 250,000 Coins (Coins) and 5 Relics.

On Amber Island, Drummidary is only available at select times. When available, it is obtained by purchasing in the Market for 40 Relics as a Vessel, which then can be revitalized by zapping eggs into it. As a Fire Quint-Elemental, Drummidary has a superb Relic production.

Song
Sample: 

Instrument: Nagadō-daiko (Taiko Drum)

On Amber Island, Drummidary plays its sound on PongPing's first verse, and then plays a thud two times on Candelavra's verse. It also plays at the end of the song with Repatillo, Rootitoot, Flum Ox, Glowl, and Gnarls, and plays a thud in the final measure.

On Light Island, Drummidary plays alongside Blow't, Yelmut, Pluckbill, Tiawa, and other Monsters near the end of the song.

On Mirror Light Island, Drummidary plays at the beginning of the song with Yelmut, Pluckbill, Flowah, and Sooza.
