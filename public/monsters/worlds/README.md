## World Art

This folder holds collection-world imagery.

Use:
- `icons/` for art shown inside the opened collection sheet
- `pins/` for art shown on the Collections home cards

Naming:
- lowercase kebab-case
- `.png`

Examples:
- `plant.png`
- `cold.png`
- `air.png`
- `water.png`
- `earth.png`
- `fire-haven.png`
- `fire-oasis.png`
- `magical-sanctum.png`
- `ethereal-island.png`
